## Cybersecurity Fundamentals

### CIA Triad

#### Confidentiality
Protecting information from unauthorized access.

#### Integrity
Ensuring data remains accurate and unchanged.

#### Availability
Making sure systems and data are available when needed.

---

## Common Cyber Threats

### Phishing
Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information.

### Malware
Malicious software designed to damage systems.

### DDoS
Flooding a target system with excessive traffic.

### SQL Injection
Injecting malicious SQL queries into applications.

### Brute Force Attack
Repeatedly guessing passwords until successful.

### Ransomware
Encrypting files and demanding payment.

---

## Attack Vectors

### Social Engineering
Manipulating people to reveal sensitive information.

### Wireless Attacks
Attacks targeting wireless networks.

### Insider Threats
Threats originating from trusted individuals.
